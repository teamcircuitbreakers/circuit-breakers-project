Simplifying Electronics for India's Next Innovators.

We are Circuit Breakers, a student-led initiative building a complete ecosystem for learning, building, and launching smart devices.

Explore Our Vision

Electronics Can Be Hard. We're Changing That.

We identified three key barriers for aspiring creators in India, and we're building a solution for each one.

Scattered Knowledge

Finding structured, practical learning paths for IoT is a challenge. We're creating a clear roadmap from beginner to expert.

Expensive Tools

High costs of sensors and gear shouldn't be a barrier. We're making resources affordable and accessible for everyone.

Idea-to-Market Gap

Brilliant projects often remain as prototypes. We're building a bridge to help creators turn their ideas into real-world products.

Our Complete Ecosystem for Innovation

Dev Section

The innovation hub where our R&D team designs, tests, and develops new smart devices, tools, and solutions. This is where ideas become reality.

Teacher Section

A dedicated space for experienced mentors specializing in coding, electronics, and IoT to share their expertise through structured lessons and hands-on training.

Student Section

Tailored for enthusiasts to dive into IoT and electronics with affordable, structured learning paths, mentorship, and practical project exposure.

Tech Library

A unique initiative to make resources accessible. For a minimal fee, access sensors, microcontrollers, books, and tools for hands-on learning.

Main Store

The commercial arm where our R&D products will be available. We will also feature creations from our community, turning ideas into market-ready solutions.

Charting Our Course

Phase 1: Our Current Focus

This is where we are right now: laying the essential groundwork for our community and platform.

  • Establishing our core online community on Discord & Telegram.
  • Creating high-value, practical tutorials on YouTube.
  • Building this foundational website to share our vision with you.

Phase 2: What's Next

Once our community is thriving, we will introduce the first pillars of our ecosystem to provide tangible value.

  • Launching the Main Store with a curated list of essential components.
  • Initiating a pilot program for the Tech Library in our local area.

Phase 3: Expanding Horizons

With the foundation set, our focus will shift to structured learning and mentorship to accelerate growth.

  • Onboarding the first mentors to our Teacher Section.
  • Launching the Student Section with affordable, project-based courses.

Phase 4: The Ultimate Vision

The final stage is to complete the loop: moving from learning to creating unique, home-grown innovations.

  • Establishing the formal R&D Dev Section for in-house projects.
  • Launching the first official "Circuit Breakers" branded product.
